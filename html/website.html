You wake up in a room, all you remember is being in the woods and someone chasing you until you see a mansion.
Will you be able to escape the masion before the thing chasing you catches up or will you surrender to the thing...

This is a text-based horror game where you must solve cryptic clues to navigate your way through the eerie, abandoned mansion. Shadows lurk in every corner, whispers echo through the halls, and every choice you make could bring you closer to the truth—or your doom. Can you uncover the mansion’s dark secrets before it’s too late?
