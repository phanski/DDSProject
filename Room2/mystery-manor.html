The Parlor

You step into a dusty, dimly lit parlor. A grand painting, an antique clock, and a curious control panel beckon you to uncover their secrets.

The centerpiece of the room is a control panel mounted on an ornate wooden cabinet. It has a series of labeled buttons and two adjustable dials. A note on the wall reads, "Only those who dare to press the right sequence and set the dials as the clock strikes truth shall unlock the path forward."
